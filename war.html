War & Defense Museums in India

Jaisalmer War Museum, Rajasthan

The Jaisalmer War Museum was conceptualised to display India's rich military history and showcase real war efforts as they happened in the past. The Jaisalmer War Museum aims to promote greater awareness of the sacrifice made by heroes of the Indian Army, in particular, and Indian Armed Forces in general.

Indian Air Force Museum, Palam

The Indian Air Force Museum Palam, is the museum of the Indian Air Force (IAF), and is located at the Palam Air Force Station in Delhi Cantonment, New Delhi, India.[1] It was established in 1967 and exhibits details about combat operations undertaken by the air force depicting its history since its formation in 1932.
