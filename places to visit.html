PLACES TO VISIT MUSEUM IN INDIA


India boasts a rich cultural heritage, and some of the best museums to explore include the National Museum in New Delhi, the Indian Museum in Kolkata, and the Salar Jung Museum in Hyderabad. Many museums have exhibitions of these objects on public display, and some have private collections that are used by researchers and specialists. Museums host a much wider range of objects than a library, and they usually focus on a specific theme, such as the arts, science, natural history or local history. Public museums that host exhibitions and interactive demonstrations are often tourist attractions, and many draw large numbers of visitors from outside of their host country, with the most visited museums in the world attracting millions of visitors annually.
